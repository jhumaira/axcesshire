FAQ

Common questions.

Here’s what most people want to know before getting started with AxcessHire.

Do you use AI to apply?

No. Every application is handled by a real person.

Do you guarantee interviews?

No. We guarantee application volume and strategic targeting, but hiring decisions belong to employers.

Will I see where you applied?

Yes. You receive a weekly application report with company names, job links, and application status.

Can I approve applications first?

Yes. You can choose full approval before submission or pre-approved criteria.

Do you apply through LinkedIn?

Yes, when appropriate. Some applications may require your login or approval.
